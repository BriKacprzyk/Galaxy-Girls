Galaxy Girls

Welcome

The Galaxy Girl's are a Monroeville based women's activity group. We are a club meant for young women ages 18-25 that want something to do on the weekend. We do activities of all kinds and are always looking for something new and exciting to do.

Meeting

We meet once a month on the second Saturday of the month. Meeting times and locations vary throughout Monroeville and surrounding locations. Only because we plan a variety of activities to do.

Want To Become A Member?

Becoming a Galaxy Girl member is easy! It's free of charge. The only fees you might pay is if we do an activity that has an entry fee such as: a fair, amusement park, or museum. Click the "Become a Member" button to fill out our membership form!
